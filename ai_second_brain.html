What hardware do I need to run it? +

The AI Second Brain runs on surprisingly modest hardware. You can use your existing laptop (Windows, macOS, Linux), a Raspberry Pi 4 (4GB+ RAM recommended), or a low-cost cloud VPS (e.g., DigitalOcean, Hetzner, Vultr for $5-10/month). It's designed to be resource-efficient while maintaining powerful capabilities.

How difficult is the setup for a non-developer? +

Think of this as building your custom-tailored digital fortress, not just installing another app. We've designed the setup process to be as streamlined as possible, even if you're not a developer. With our clear, step-by-step guides and simplified Docker-based scripts, you can get the core AI Second Brain operational and interacting with your messaging bot in just 2-3 hours. That's your first major win! The primary time investment (typically 2-4 hours for most users) is a one-time process for securely connecting your specific personal accounts (like your Telegram bot and Google Calendar/Email). This crucial step ensures your data remains 100% private and under your control. Consider this initial setup as a permanent investment in digital freedom, freeing you from endless subscriptions and fragmented cloud tools. Once your AI Second Brain is running, it's yours to continually expand and personalize with optional, rewarding automations—no ongoing technical effort required for daily use. We provide all the support you need to make it happen.

What about my data privacy? +

Your data never leaves your control. Everything runs locally on your hardware or your chosen private server. There are no cloud dependencies for core operations, no third-party access, and absolutely no data mining. This is true privacy by design.

Is this a subscription service? +

Absolutely not. This is a one-time purchase. You own the software and the blueprint forever. There are no monthly fees, no hidden costs, and no subscription traps. The only ongoing cost is your choice of hardware/hosting, which can be very low (e.g., electricity for a Raspberry Pi or a $5 VPS).

Yes! The system includes a powerful, flexible YAML-based workflow engine that allows you to define custom automations similar to how Zapier works.
